About Nebula IT Solutions

Founded in 2019, Nebula IT Solutions is a dynamic and forward-thinking educational and technological institution based in Abudwak, Galmudug region, Somalia. For over 6 years, we have been delivering quality IT solutions and training programs that empower businesses and individuals.

Our mission is to bridge the technology gap in Somalia by providing cutting-edge IT services, comprehensive training programs, and strategic consulting that drives digital transformation and business growth.

At Nebula IT Solutions, we combine technical expertise with local understanding to deliver solutions that meet the unique challenges and opportunities of the Somali market.

Whether you need custom software development, cybersecurity solutions, cloud migration, or IT training, our team of experts is committed to delivering excellence and driving your success.
